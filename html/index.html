Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is Peaceful Path?

Peaceful Path is a platform designed to help you find peace and balance through mental wellness tools, including meditation, relaxation, mood tracking, and more.

How do I start meditation sessions?

You can start meditation sessions by clicking on the "Start Meditation" button on the homepage or navigating to the Meditation & Relaxation section.

What is Daily Wellness?

Daily Wellness provides you with simple tips and practices that you can incorporate into your routine to improve your mental well-being.

How can I track my mental progress?

You can track your mood and mental health by visiting the "Mood & Mental Tracking" section and recording your emotions and thoughts.
